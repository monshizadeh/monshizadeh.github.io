Biography & Profile

Hossein Monshizadeh Naeen received his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering in 2019. He also received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Engineering in 2010 and 2013, respectively. He is an assistant professor at the Islamic Azad University (IAU) of Neyshabur, Neyshabur, Iran. From 2021 to 2023, he served as the Head of the Department of Computer Engineering at IAU-Neyshabur, leading academic initiatives and departmental development during this period.

Dr. Monshizadeh has published extensively in areas including virtual machine consolidation and resource allocation in cloud computing, machine learning for IoT intrusion and anomaly detection, climate change impact assessment, and emerging topics such as metaverse-driven systems and explainable AI. His recent publications reflect a strong concentration on secure, intelligent, and efficient computing systems.
