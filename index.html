Biography

Hossein Monshizadeh Naeen received his Ph.D. in Computer Engineering in 2019. He also received his B.Sc. and M.Sc. degrees in Computer Engineering in 2010 and 2013, respectively. He is an assistant professor at the Islamic Azad University (IAU) of Neyshabur, Neyshabur, Iran. Currently, He is the head of the Department of Computer Engineering at IAU-Neyshabur. His current research interests include Fog & Cloud Computing, Internet of Things, Computer Networks, Data Mining, and Stochastic Processes.
